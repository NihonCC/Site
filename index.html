Welcome to Nihon Culture Club

We are a Community based in Amity University Haryana.
At Nihon Culture Club, we celebrate the rich and diverse culture of Japan.
Whether you're a lifelong Japan enthusiast or just curious about the Land of the Rising Sun, our club offers something for everyone.
Join us as we explore the traditions, arts, cuisine, language, and contemporary life of Japan.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote understanding and appreciation of Japanese culture through engaging and educational activities.
We strive to create a welcoming environment where members can share their passion for Japan, make new friends, and learn something new every day.

What We Offer

Cultural Events: Experience traditional Japanese festivals, karaokes, and calligraphy workshops. Our events are designed to immerse you in the beauty and elegance of Japanese traditions.

Language Classes: Learn to speak, read, and write Japanese with our beginner and advanced language courses. Our experienced instructors provide a supportive and interactive learning environment.

Culinary Adventures: Discover the art of Japanese cuisine through cooking classes, food tastings, and restaurant outings. From sushi-making to exploring regional dishes, you'll get a taste of Japan's culinary delights.

Anime & Manga: Dive into the world of Japanese animation and comics. Join our screenings, discussions, and drawing workshops to explore this vibrant aspect of Japanese pop culture.

Cultural Discussions: Engage in thought-provoking discussions on various aspects of Japanese society, history, and contemporary issues. Our guest speakers and members share insights that deepen our understanding of Japan.

Join Us

Becoming a member of Nihon Culture Club is easy and open to everyone. We welcome you to join our community. Membership includes access to all club events, classes, and resources.

Contact Us
